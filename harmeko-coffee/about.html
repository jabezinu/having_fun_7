Our Story

Discover the passion and dedication behind Harmeko Coffee's exceptional coffee experience

How It All Started

Founded in 2015 by master barista James Harmeko, our coffee shop began with a simple mission: to serve exceptional coffee in an elegant setting. James, who spent over a decade studying coffee cultivation and roasting techniques across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Italy, returned home with a vision to create something truly special.

What started as a small artisanal coffee bar quickly gained recognition for its extraordinary flavor profiles and sophisticated ambiance. The combination of carefully sourced beans, meticulous brewing methods, and unwavering attention to detail earned Harmeko Coffee a dedicated following among coffee enthusiasts and gastronomes alike.

As our reputation grew, so did our offerings. We expanded our menu to include gourmet cuisine that complemented our coffee, creating a harmonious culinary experience that delights all the senses.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Harmeko Coffee

Excellence

We pursue excellence in every aspect of our business, from sourcing the finest beans to crafting the perfect cup. Mediocrity has no place in our establishment.

Ethical Sourcing

We maintain direct relationships with coffee farmers, ensuring fair compensation and sustainable practices. Our success should never come at the expense of others.

Sustainability

From eco-friendly packaging to energy-efficient operations, we strive to minimize our environmental footprint while maximizing positive impact.

Community

We believe in creating a welcoming space where people can connect, converse, and create memories. Our café is more than a business; it's a community.

Innovation

While respecting traditional coffee craft, we continuously explore new techniques, flavors, and experiences to surprise and delight our guests.

Passion

Above all, we are driven by passion—for exceptional coffee, culinary artistry, and creating moments of joy for everyone who walks through our doors.
